C — 01

Aether

v2.0 Cognitive System
Active — Internal

Aether is RICKCLMNT's proprietary cognitive AI system — the internal engine that powers all our products. Built on the 8R Cognitive Framework, it separates the act of reasoning from the act of language generation. Where conventional AI systems generate responses based on pattern prediction, Aether reasons structurally first, producing outputs anchored in traceable logic.

Reasoning before language. Aether's core design principle is the separation of the reasoning pass from the generation pass. The system builds a structured cognitive representation of a problem before constructing a response — enabling it to hold position, identify contradictions, and adapt under uncertainty rather than confabulating fluently.

Semantic Knowledge Store

SQLite-backed local knowledge base with domain-aware seeding across science, AI/CS, and biochemistry — with 30-relation semantic parsing.

Structural Analogy Engine

Encoded archetypes that enable novel hypothesis generation through structural analogy — not retrieval, but genuine inference.

26 Active Cognitive Modules

A fully wired modular architecture spanning perception, reasoning, memory, and generation — purpose-built for integrated AI cognition.

PAL Deployment Layer

Aether deployed with a character layer — a full voice, identity, and interaction model built for real-world companion contexts.
