Architecture

How AETHER
actually works.


AETHER is a deterministic reasoning engine. It answers questions, solves problems, completes tasks for users by processing language and applying structured logic — without a large language model anywhere in its core loop.

Hallucination in a large language model is not a bug. It is a structural consequence of the architecture: the model generates the statistically most probable continuation of the input, and sometimes the most probable continuation is factually wrong. The model has no mechanism to distinguish between "I know this" and "this sounds right."

AETHER avoids that entirely with its deterministic architecture, designed to eliminate hallucination.
