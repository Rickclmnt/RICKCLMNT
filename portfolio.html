Privacy Infrastructure · 2026

PhantomSpace

A virtual Android environment that lives inside an app — a second phone, cryptographically separate from your real one, with its own apps, network identity, and device fingerprint. The vault is encrypted with AES-256-XTS and unrecoverable without the PIN. All traffic routes through Tor with a hard kill switch, and a duress PIN destroys the vault on command.
